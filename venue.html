Venue

    The conference will take place in the Konferenzraum of the faculty of mathematics and computer science of Heidelberg University. The faculty is located in the campus "Neuenheimer Feld", building number 205, the building opposite to bus station "Bunsengymnasium West".


    View Larger Map


    How to reach the campus "Neuenheimer Feld"

    Coming from Frankfurt Airport:

    • Take a train from the German Railway to Heidelberg Main Station (Hauptbahnhof).
    • Use the Airport Transfer and Limousine Service.
      (TLS; Tel. +49 (0)6221-770077)
      Depending on which airline you are using, you will be picked up at the Meeting Point at either Terminal 1 or 2. TLS will take you directly to the Institute or any address you have given by booking. Reservation - best until 48 h in advance - with information on the airline and flight number is required.
