Registration

    SEA 2022 registration is now open. For all questions on registration, please email us at christian.schulz@informatik.uni-heidelberg.de.

    Early registration deadline is 13th June 2022. Registration closes on 8th July 2022.

    At least one author of each accepted paper must register by the deadline of the early registration using a regular registration fee, and give a presentation of his/her paper at the conference. One early regular registration fee must be paid for each accepted paper. Available fees are as follows.

    Registration Fees Early (on or before June 13th) Late (after June 13th)
    Student €290 €340
    Regular €340 €390


    The fee covers the following:
    Welcome reception
    Attendance to all sessions and invited talks
    Coffee breaks
    Lunches
    Social Event and Conference Banquet

    The registration fees also cover (among others) the LIPIcs publication costs. Extra tickets covering Social Event and Conference Banquet for accompanying people are available and will be sold separately.

    Payment is by credit card or by wire transfer at the following registration link. For any question on the registration procedure, please email us at christian.schulz@informatik.uni-heidelberg.de. Student registration covers the same as Regular registration. Note that a participant must be a full-time student in order to benefit from the reduced fee (certificate or equivalent proof will have to be uploaded during the registration process).
